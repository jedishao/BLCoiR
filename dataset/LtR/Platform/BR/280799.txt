Bug 280799  [Commands] ActivateEditorHandler unnecessarily calls setFocus()Build id: I20090603-2000

According to setFocus()'s javadoc...

Clients should not call this method (the workbench calls this method at appropriate times). To have the workbench activate a part, use IWorkbenchPage.activate(IWorkbenchPart) instead.

In ActivateEditorHandler, activate(IWorkbenchPart) is invoked quickly followed by a setFocus() invocation on lines 57 and 58. From the stack trace below, setFocus() is already called when activate(IWorkbenchPart) is invoked, so the explicit call to setFocus() appears to serve no purpose.
